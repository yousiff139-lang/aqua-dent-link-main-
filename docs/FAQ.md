# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Dental Care Connect?

Dental Care Connect is an online platform that allows patients to browse dentist profiles, book appointments, and manage their dental care appointments conveniently from any device.

---

### Do I need an account to use the platform?

Yes, you need to create a free account to book appointments. This allows you to:
- View your appointment history
- Manage upcoming appointments
- Receive email confirmations and reminders
- Store your contact information for faster booking

Creating an account is quick and only requires an email address and password.

---

### Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take security seriously:
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We use industry-standard security practices
- Payment information is processed securely through Stripe
- We never store your credit card details
- Your information is never shared with third parties without consent
- We comply with HIPAA and data protection regulations

---

### Is the platform free to use?

Yes, creating an account and browsing dentist profiles is completely free. You only pay for the dental services you book, either online through Stripe or in cash at the clinic.

---

## Booking Appointments

### How do I book an appointment?

1. Sign in to your account (or create one)
2. Browse the Dentists page
3. Click "View Profile" to see dentist details
4. Click "Book Appointment"
5. Fill out the booking form
6. Choose your payment method
7. Submit and receive confirmation

See our [Patient Booking Guide](./PATIENT_BOOKING_GUIDE.md) for detailed instructions.

---

### How far in advance can I book?

You can book appointments up to 3 months in advance. Same-day appointments are not available through online booking. For urgent appointments, please call the clinic directly.

---

### Can I book multiple appointments at once?

Currently, you need to book appointments one at a time. After completing one booking, you can immediately start another booking for a different date or dentist.

---

### What if my preferred time slot is not available?

If your preferred time is unavailable:
- Try a different time on the same day
- Check availability on nearby dates
- Try booking earlier in the day (morning slots often have more availability)
- Contact the clinic directly to inquire about additional availability
- Check back later as cancellations may open up slots

---

### Can I book for someone else (child, family member)?

Yes, you can book appointments for family members:
- Use your account to make the booking
- Enter the patient's name and contact information in the form
- Specify in the "Reason for Visit" that you're booking for someone else
- You'll receive the confirmation, but the appointment will be under the patient's name

For children under 18, a parent or guardian must accompany them to the appointment.

---

### What information do I need to book?

You'll need:
- Patient's full name
- Email address (for confirmation)
- Phone number
- Preferred appointment date and time
- Reason for visit / chief complaint
- Payment method (credit card or cash)

---

### Do I need to provide insurance information when booking?

Insurance information is not required during online booking. You can provide insurance details when you arrive at the clinic. The booking system focuses on scheduling; billing and insurance are handled separately at the clinic.

---

## Payment Questions

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept two payment methods:

**1. Credit/Debit Card (Stripe):**
- Pay online when booking
- Secure payment processing
- Immediate confirmation
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover

**2. Cash:**
- Pay at the clinic during your appointment
- No online payment required
- Appointment still confirmed
- Bring exact amount or change

---

### When do I pay for my appointment?

**If you choose Stripe:**
- Pay immediately when booking
- Secure online payment
- Receive instant confirmation

**If you choose Cash:**
- Pay at the clinic before or after your appointment
- No upfront payment required
- Appointment is still reserved

---

### Is online payment secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use Stripe, a leading payment processor trusted by millions of businesses worldwide. Stripe is:
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified (highest security standard)
- Used by companies like Amazon, Google, and Shopify
- Encrypted with bank-level security
- We never see or store your card details

---

### Will I receive a receipt?

Yes:
- **Stripe payments:** Immediate email receipt from Stripe
- **Cash payments:** Receipt provided at the clinic
- All receipts include appointment details and payment amount
- Receipts can be used for insurance reimbursement

---

### What if my payment fails?

If your online payment fails:
1. Check your card details are correct
2. Ensure sufficient funds are available
3. Try a different card
4. Contact your bank if the issue persists
5. Choose "Cash" payment option as an alternative

Your appointment slot is held for 10 minutes while you resolve payment issues.

---

### Can I get a refund?

Refund policy depends on when you cancel:

**More than 24 hours before appointment:**
- Full refund for Stripe payments
- Processed within 5-7 business days

**Less than 24 hours before appointment:**
- 50% cancellation fee applies
- 50% refunded to original payment method

**No-show (missed appointment):**
- No refund
- Full charge applies

**Cash payments:**
- No refund needed (payment not yet made)
- Cancellation fees may apply for future bookings

---

## Managing Appointments

### How do I view my appointments?

1. Sign in to your account
2. Click on your name in the navigation bar
3. Select "My Appointments" or "Dashboard"
4. View all upcoming and past appointments

You can also access appointments from the confirmation email link.

---

### Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

**To cancel:**
1. Go to your Patient Dashboard
2. Find the appointment
3. Click "Cancel Appointment"
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Receive cancellation confirmation email

**To reschedule:**
1. Cancel the existing appointment
2. Book a new appointment with your preferred date/time
3. Or contact the clinic to reschedule directly

**Note:** Cancellation policy applies (see refund section above).

---

### How do I know my appointment is confirmed?

You'll receive confirmation in multiple ways:
1. Confirmation page immediately after booking
2. Confirmation email with appointment details
3. PDF attachment with appointment information
4. Calendar invite (.ics file) to add to your calendar
5. Appointment visible in your Patient Dashboard

---

### Will I receive appointment reminders?

Yes, you'll receive:
- Email reminder 24 hours before appointment
- SMS reminder (if phone number provided)
- Email reminder 1 hour before appointment (optional)

Make sure your email and phone number are correct in your account settings.

---

### What if I need to arrive late?

If you'll be late:
1. Call the clinic as soon as possible
2. Inform them of your expected arrival time
3. They'll do their best to accommodate you
4. Appointments more than 15 minutes late may need to be rescheduled

---

### What if I miss my appointment?

If you miss your appointment without canceling:
- You'll be charged the full appointment fee
- No refund will be issued
- You'll need to book a new appointment
- Repeated no-shows may result in booking restrictions

Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance if you cannot attend.

---

## Dentist Profiles

### How do I find a dentist?

**Browse all dentists:**
1. Go to the Dentists page
2. View all available dentists
3. See ratings, specializations, and photos

**Search/Filter (if available):**
- Filter by specialization
- Sort by rating or experience
- Search by name

**View detailed profiles:**
- Click "View Profile" on any dentist card
- Read full bio, education, and expertise
- See patient reviews and ratings

---

### What information is shown on dentist profiles?

Each profile includes:
- Name and photo
- Specialization (e.g., General Dentistry, Orthodontics)
- Years of experience
- Star rating (out of 5)
- Professional biography
- Education and qualifications
- Areas of expertise
- Available appointment times
- Contact information

---

### Are dentist ratings real?

Yes, ratings are based on actual patient reviews. Patients can rate their experience after appointments. We verify that ratings come from real patients who had appointments.

---

### Can I choose a specific dentist?

Yes, you can choose any dentist from our network. Browse profiles, read reviews, and select the dentist that best fits your needs. You can also book with different dentists for different appointments.

---

### What if a dentist is not available?

If a dentist shows no available times:
- They may be fully booked for the visible date range
- Try selecting dates further in the future
- Check back later for cancellations
- Choose a different dentist with similar expertise
- Contact the clinic to inquire about availability

---

### Can I see a dentist's schedule before booking?

Yes, when you view a dentist's profile, you can see their available time slots. This helps you choose a convenient time before starting the booking process.

## Account Questions

1. Click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar

3. Create a secure password

1. Click "Sign In"

3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password
6. Sign in with your new password

---

### Can I change my email address?

Yes:
1. Sign in to your account
2. Go to Account Settings or Profile
3. Update your email address
4. Verify the new email address
5. Use the new email for future sign-ins

---

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:
1. Contact support at support@dentalcareconnect.com
2. Request account deletion
3. Confirm your identity
4. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

**Note:** You cannot delete your account if you have upcoming appointments. Cancel all appointments first.

---

### Can I have multiple accounts?

We recommend using only one account per person. Multiple accounts can cause confusion with appointments and medical records. If you need to manage appointments for family members, you can do so from a single account.

## Privacy and Security

### What data do you collect?

We collect:
- Account information (name, email, password)
- Contact information (phone number)
- Appointment details (date, time, reason for visit)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe, not stored by us)
- Usage data (pages visited, features used)

Your information is visible to:

## Billing and Insurance

### Do you accept dental insurance?

Insurance acceptance varies by clinic and dentist. The online booking system does not handle insurance directly. To use insurance:
1. Book your appointment online
2. Bring your insurance card to the appointment
3. The clinic will verify coverage and process claims
4. You pay any remaining balance at the clinic

Contact the clinic directly to verify if they accept your insurance.

---

### How much does an appointment cost?

Costs vary by:
- Type of appointment
- Dentist and clinic
- Your insurance coverage
- Specific procedures needed

The booking system does not display prices. Contact the clinic for pricing information or check with your insurance provider for covered services.

---

### Can I get an invoice for my appointment?

Yes:
- Stripe payments: Automatic email receipt
- Cash payments: Request invoice at clinic
- For insurance: Clinic provides detailed invoice
- For reimbursement: Request itemized receipt
