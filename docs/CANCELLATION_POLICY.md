## Exceptions and Special Cases

### Emergency Situations

**Medical Emergencies:**
- Call the clinic immediately
- Explain your situation
- They will accommodate you

**Family Emergencies:**
- Contact clinic as soon as possible
- Provide brief explanation
- Clinic may waive late cancellation policy

**Examples:**
- Sudden illness
- Family member hospitalization
- Car accident
- Natural disaster
- Other genuine emergencies

### Weather-Related Cancellations

**Severe Weather:**
- Clinic may cancel appointments
- You'll be notified immediately
- No penalty for weather cancellations
- Rescheduling assistance provided

**Examples:**
- Snowstorms
- Hurricanes
- Flooding
- Extreme heat advisories

### Clinic-Initiated Cancellations

**Dentist Unavailable:**
- Clinic will notify you ASAP
- No penalty to you
- Priority rescheduling offered
- Apology and explanation provided

**Reasons:**
- Dentist illness
- Emergency procedures
- Equipment failure
- Facility issues

### Multiple Cancellations

**Pattern of Cancellations:**

If you cancel frequently:
- 3+ cancellations in 3 months may trigger review
- Clinic may require phone booking instead of online
- May require deposit for future appointments
- Account may be flagged for review

**To avoid issues:**
- Only book when you're reasonably sure you can attend
- Cancel as early as possible
- Communicate with clinic about scheduling challenges

## Consequences of No-Shows

### What is a No-Show?

A no-show occurs when you:
- Don't arrive for your appointment
- Don't cancel in advance
- Don't contact the clinic

### No-Show Policy

**First No-Show:**
- Warning issued
- Reminder about cancellation policy
- No financial penalty

**Second No-Show:**
- Account flagged
- May require phone confirmation for future bookings
- Possible deposit requirement

**Third No-Show:**
- May result in account suspension
- Require in-person registration for future appointments
- Possible no-show fee

### Avoiding No-Shows

‚úÖ **Set Reminders:**
- Add appointment to calendar
- Set phone reminders
- Enable notification alerts

‚úÖ **Plan Ahead:**
- Check transportation
- Arrange time off work
- Confirm childcare if needed

‚úÖ **Cancel Early:**
- If you can't make it, cancel ASAP
- Don't wait until the last minute
- Help others get the time slot

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What if I'm running late but can still make it?**
A: Call the clinic immediately. They may be able to accommodate you or reschedule.

**Q: Can I cancel someone else's appointment?**
A: No, only the person who booked can cancel. Contact clinic for family member cancellations.

**Q: What if I cancel by mistake?**
A: Book a new appointment immediately. If the slot is taken, contact the clinic for help.

**Q: Do I get a refund if I cancel?**
A: If you haven't paid yet, there's nothing to refund. If you've paid a deposit, contact the clinic.

### Timing Questions

**Q: How is the 1-hour window calculated?**
A: From the current time to your appointment start time. The system calculates this automatically.

**Q: What if my appointment is in exactly 1 hour?**
A: You cannot cancel through the system. Call the clinic.

**Q: Can I cancel the night before?**
A: Yes! Cancelling the night before is encouraged and gives the clinic time to fill the slot.

**Q: What time zone is used?**
A: Your local time zone. The system adjusts automatically.

### Emergency Questions

**Q: What if I have a medical emergency?**
A: Your health comes first. Seek medical care, then contact the clinic when possible.

**Q: What if my car breaks down on the way?**
A: Call the clinic immediately. They'll understand and help reschedule.

**Q: What if I'm sick on appointment day?**
A: Call the clinic. They may prefer you reschedule to avoid spreading illness.

### Policy Questions

**Q: Why can't I cancel within 1 hour?**
A: It's too late for the clinic to fill the slot. This policy respects the dentist's time.

**Q: What if the clinic cancels on me?**
A: No penalty to you. They'll help you reschedule at your convenience.

**Q: Can the policy be waived?**
A: Only the clinic can waive the policy. Contact them directly for special circumstances.

**Q: Is this policy legal?**
A: Yes, cancellation policies are standard in healthcare and service industries.
