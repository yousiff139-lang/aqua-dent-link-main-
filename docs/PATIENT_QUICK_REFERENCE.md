## üí≥ Payment Quick Reference

### Payment Methods
- **Credit/Debit Card (Stripe)**: Pay online, instant confirmation
- **Cash**: Pay at clinic, no upfront payment

### When to Pay
- **Stripe**: When booking (immediate)
- **Cash**: At clinic (before/after appointment)

### Refund Policy
- **24+ hours before**: Full refund
- **Less than 24 hours**: 50% fee
- **No-show**: No refund

**Full details:** [Payment FAQ](FAQ.md#payment-questions)

## üìß Email Quick Reference

### Expected Emails
- Account verification (when signing up)
- Booking confirmation (after booking)
- Appointment reminder (24 hours before)
- Cancellation confirmation (if cancelled)
- Password reset (if requested)

### Not Receiving Emails?
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 10-15 minutes
3. Verify email address is correct
4. Whitelist noreply@dentalcareconnect.com
5. Contact support
